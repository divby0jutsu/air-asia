    Personalized tour of Vietnam

    We did a trip to Vietnam from February 9th to March 10th with arrangements made through Air Asia. We booked with them after my sister who has used them a few times recommended them. Our agent Ha Hai was amazing and helped us plan a trip of a lifetime. We toured from North to South and while planning told our agent what we wanted to see. Ha Hai booked all our internal flights and accommodations checking with us to make sure we were good with the various hotels and locations. We could not have had a more smooth trip from pick ups at the airport to drop off at our hotels with many, many adventures in between. Watching how rice paper is made and then making it, cooking classes where we learned how to make our favorite spring rolls both fried and fresh, boat tours, ,snorkeling, spice farms, the list just goes on and on. The tour usually included breakfasts and many lunches which we enjoyed as they took us to many areas to eat that were fantastic.
